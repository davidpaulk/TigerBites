TigerBites notifies users via email when their “favorited” dining items are served at one of the dining halls. Emails are sent 1-2 hours before each meal. Users can view daily menus on our website, and afer logging in, can also add dining items to their lists of favorites by clicking a plus sign beside a dining item. Users can also search items from all past dining items to add to or remove from their favorites. Under "favorites" tab, users can easily manage their favorited items.
